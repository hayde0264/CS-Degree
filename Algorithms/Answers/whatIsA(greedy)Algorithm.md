# What is a greedy algorithm? 

A greedy algorithm computes solutions in steps. During such steps, choices are made that are considered **"optimum"**. 


## Famous examples: 
- **Shortest Path Algorithm** 
- **Prim's Algorithm** 
- **Kruskal's Algoritym** 
- **Huffman Coding**
