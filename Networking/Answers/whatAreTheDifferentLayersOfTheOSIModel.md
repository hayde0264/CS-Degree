# What are the different layers of the OSI model? 

## The Open Systems Interconnect model is broken into seven layers: 
7. **Application** 
6. **Presentation** 
5. **Session** 
4. **Transport** 
3. **Network** 
2. **Data Link** 
1. **Physical**
